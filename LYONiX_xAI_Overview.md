# LYONiX Suite - Overview for xAI

**A lightweight, graph-based provenance system for the AI era**

## Goal
Create tamper-proof records of digital content (especially AI-generated) to prove originality, track derivatives, and enable fair attribution.

## Current Capabilities
- Monad Core with cryptographic hashing and timestamps (LYO units)
- Working demo showing provenance creation
- Tiered architecture ready for expansion

## Tier Roadmap
- **Tier 1**: Core provenance (done)
- **Tier 2**: Graph-based lineage tracking
- **Tier 3**: Semantic similarity & derivative detection
- **Tier 4+**: Disagreement engines, reasoning support, AI risk & compliance tools

## Why Relevant to xAI
- Better transparency for training data
- Tools to reduce uncredited derivatives
- Potential to improve model truthfulness and reasoning
- Lightweight and local-first design
